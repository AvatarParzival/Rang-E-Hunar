Our Story

Rang-E-Hunar is a thoughtfully curated collection of timeless, hand-painted pieces inspired by our rich culture and the beauty of nature. We believe in the art of handmade craftsmanship, quality, and creating pieces that tell a meaningful story..

Each item in our collection is carefully selected, whether it’s a hand-painted dupatta or a classic shirt designed with nature’s elegance in mind.

"Our Classic T-Shirt: Rose "

Mission

After observing the social dynamics and limited opportunities available to many girls in Pakistan, a group of passionate individuals came together to form what is now known as Rang-E-Hunar. While our focus is on delivering premium-quality, hand-painted garments rooted in tradition and nature, our deeper purpose lies in empowerment.

"Our Trendy Denim Cap: Blossom"

Purpose

Rang-E-Hunar was created to give talented young girls a platform: a space where they can express themselves, earn with dignity, and tell their stories through color and craft. We believe in the strength of youth and the untapped potential of girls who simply need a chance to be seen.
